To Publish New app Version Of already existing Published app on Google
Play Console.
So, U must have an Updated '.aab' file for Of Your App.
1. On 'build.gradle.kts' in 'app' folder U have to Upgrade the
"versionCode" and 'versionName' from it's previous version +1.
2. Now we have to build the 'signed APK bundle'. So go to "menu"
then 'build' Now select the 'Generate Signed App Bundle or APK'.
Choose 'AAB' then make Sure u choose the "Previous released Version"
of your app which is already there.
Choose existing one and keep password same. 
Click on 'next' and 'release'.
3. Now 'Generate Signed Bundle' is Create U can see on 'Right Corner'
pop up click on 'locate'.
   NOW U GOT '.AAB' FILE WHICH WE WENT TO UPLOAD NOW NAVIGATE TO
          GOOGLE PLAY CONSOLE OVER EXISTING APPLICATION
                  DASHBOARD:
1. On Right Slider We Got Options At which we have to choose 
'Test and Release' At Which we Got Sub options.
We Choose 2nd option called 'Production'.
2. Inside Production we got Option of 'Release Dashboard'
Then Now click on 'Create New Release'.
Now Here If u scroll Down Got An Option 'App Bundle'.
So, Upload Your 'App Bundle'.
3. When Your "App Bundle" Is Uploaded U See it's 'name' 
and 'Version'.
     ---------------Note----------------
If u Scroll down U See 'Release Details' of previous Version.
At WHich U can Modify 'Release Notes' For Being Professional.
     ----------------X------------------
4. Now On SideBar we have To Choose 'Monitor and improve'
click on it then click on 'Policy and Program' then Go to 
'App Content' At which On Screen We got two Option Choose 
'Actioned' On which we have to manage permission According to
App requirement.
5. Now on SideBar Choose 'Test and Release' show previous version
of our release. Choose Sub Option 'Production' here we go to 
'releases' option Then scroll down u see the previous version.
Click on 'Manage' option.
